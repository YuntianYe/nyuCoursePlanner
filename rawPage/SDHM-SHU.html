SDHM-SHU 410 Self-Designed Honors Major Capstone Seminar

This seminar introduces self-designed honors majors to research methods in preparation for the Capstone Honors Independent Study in the spring semester of the senior year. By the end of the course, students will have produced a well-formulated research question, methodological design, and bibliography. Open only to seniors who have been approved as self-designed honors majors.
