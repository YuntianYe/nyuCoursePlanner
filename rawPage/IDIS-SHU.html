IDIS-SHU 997 Independent Study - Interdisciplinary

Prerequisite: permission of the Dean and Assistant Provost for Academic Affairs. 2 or 4 credits. Students are permitted to work on an individual basis under the supervision of a full-time faculty member in the academic discipline if they have maintained an overall GPA of 3.0 and have a study proposal that is approved by the professor. Students are expected to spend about ten to twelve hours a week on their project for 4 credits. The results of the study are embodied in a report of a type required by the instructor.
